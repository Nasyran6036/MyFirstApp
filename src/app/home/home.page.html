KL2017: Malaysia on its way to becoming first Sea Games cricket champions

KUALA LUMPUR: Malaysia took a big step towards becoming cricket's first Sea Games champions after beating Singapore by six wickets in the 50-overs competition at the Kinrara Oval on Wednesday. Ahmad Faiz Mohammad Noor's century led Malaysia to a victory which ensures the hosts will finish top of round-robin table by winning their final match against winless Indonesia on Thursday.
